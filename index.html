About Me

Spunky. Driven. Funny. A little obsessed with weight lifting. At times a bit too honest. Those are just some of the words I use when asked to describe myself. I love a good joke, soccer, classic novels, and hiking, but my all-time favorite way to spend my time is making memories with my husband and 3 kids (ages 7, 4, and 1).

Professionally I started out as an educator, teaching at a rural elementary school in Northern Utah. It was fulfilling, fun, and challenging. I stopped teaching when my husband’s schooling took us overseas to Israel for two years. Upon return to the States I began my own health coaching business. I have loved giving people the tools they need to successfully meet their goals. I’ve taken that passion and applied it to web development. Now I create the tools needed to help both businesses and individuals succeed.
